This site is a short overview of the 2025 F1 season. It is meant to show the final standings, current driver's profiles and information on this season's tracks.

Styling
The site's design is based on a color pallet similar to that of the F1 logo. Furthermore, info boxes' styling is a refence to F1 soft tyres.

Information Disclaimer
Although information was gathered and summarized with great care, this site is not an official source of information and is not affiliated with the FIA whatsoever.
